Summary

The views folder  typically serves as the location for storing templates that are used to generate HTML pages dynamically.
These templates are a fundamental part of web application 

Layouts
This folder typically contains layout templates.
Layout templates define the overall structure of a web page,
including common elements like headers, footers, navigation menus, 
and placeholders for dynamic content. Layouts are used to ensure a consistent look and feel across multiple pages of your website.

Partials
Partials are smaller, reusable templates that can be included within other templates. 
They are often used for components or sections of a page that appear on multiple pages.
